â–¼information
8GB Ram is recommended for running Overloaded 1.20.1.

The Wilted can destroy blocks with some of its attacks, claiming chunks can
prevent this and staying underground is the best method to avoid the creature
entirely.

Chunks can be claimed by opening the inventory and clicking on the FTB
chunks icon located at the top left side of the REI (Roughly Enough Items) gui.

Once you've entered The End, the Ender Dragon boss fight can be activated by
getting close to the Bell Tower in the center of the main end island.

The Ender Dragon can be respawned by placing a crystal on the 4 bedrock blocks
that can be found within the tower, one in each directional wing of the building.

â–¼settings

If Overloaded is set to Full Screen, it will crash or refuse to respond when
the game freezes during these instances:

During the "Preparing for world creation..." screen,
when loading into a world or server,
on first opening of inventory after joining a world,
potentially other unknown instances.

Setting your window to Windowed or Borderless allows the
game to respond and not crash during these instances.



Setting "Mipmap Levels" above 0x may cause a massive dip in performance
depending on your device due to the Point Blank mod.

Setting "Simulation Distance" above 12 chunks can create lag and/or
increase the internal server's load.
